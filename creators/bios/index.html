Creators

Chris Scott

Shooting photography & film for more than two decades and working with editors, art directors, designers across lifestyle, interiors & fashion, Chris has connected with some of the most exciting brands, including BBH, DKNY, Puma, V&A, Issey Miyake & Blain Southern to name a few.

In 2013, Chris began teaching photography to the next generation of creative leaders at the prestigious London College of Fashion.

The idea to create a precise and distinctive platform for exceptional weddings came from two commissions undertaken the following year, as well as the realisation that a very talented make up designer shared his vision.

Mikaela Honeywill

Mikaela has over 20 years experience as a make up artist, working in music, fashion, commercial & advertising and with key figures in the arts & entertainment world.

She has made up many famous faces including Lady Kitty, Eliza & Amelia Spencer (for the Royal Wedding 2011), Brian Ferry, Nick Cave, Stella Tennant, Banksy, Piper Perabo, Elton John, as well as having worked with many high profile brands including, Agent Provocateur, Nike, Citroen, Virgin, Tallulah & Hope and creating looks for London Fashion Week.

Her editorial work has appeared in publications such as Dazed and Confused, The Guardian, GQ, The Telegraph and I-D magazine and she has worked with many high profile photographers such as Rankin, Greg Williams and Lorenzo Agius.

With all her expertise and versatility, Mikaela is now very excited about the prospect of delivering the ultimate experience for AfterglowBrides.
